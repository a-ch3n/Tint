03 / BENEFITS
Why tint

Comfort, protection, presence.

Heat Rejection

Ceramic film blocks up to 80% of infrared radiation. Your AC works half as hard, cabin temps drop 20°F+ on a hot day.

UV Protection

99% UV-A and UV-B blocked. Skin protected on long drives, interiors stop fading, leather and dashboards last decades.

Glare Control

Snow glare, headlight glare, low-sun glare — gone. Less squinting, less eye fatigue, sharper focus on the road.

Privacy

Valuables stay invisible. A darker cabin is a quieter cabin — psychologically and acoustically.

Shatter Resistance

Tinted glass holds together in an impact. The film acts as a structural membrane — broken glass stays put.

Lifetime Warranty

No bubbling, no peeling, no purple-fade. If our film ever fails for the life of the car, we replace it free.

05 / FAQ
Questions, answered

Before you book.

Minnesota requires passenger car front side windows to allow at least 50% VLT (visible light transmission). Rear and back windows on SUVs, vans, and trucks have far more flexibility. We tint to spec and tell you exactly where your build sits before we cut a single panel.
A two-window job runs about 45 minutes. A full vehicle is typically 2.5–3.5 hours depending on glass geometry. We schedule in blocks so you're never waiting for someone else's job to finish.
Carbon is our standard film — deep matte look, real heat rejection, color-stable, and never fades to purple. Ceramic is the upgrade ($80 more on any package): nano-particle construction that blocks 80% of infrared without metal, so your phone, GPS, and tire-pressure sensors all work normally. For Minnesota's summer sun and winter snow-glare we recommend ceramic on full-vehicle jobs.
Drivable in a few hours. Fully cured in 3–7 days in warm weather, up to 2 weeks in winter. You'll see some haze and water pockets during cure — that's normal and it clears. Don't roll the windows down for at least 3 days.
Not with our films. Older metalized tints used to block GPS, cell signal, and toll transponders. Modern ceramic film is metal-free — zero interference with anything inside the cabin.
Lifetime warranty against bubbling, peeling, cracking, delaminating, and color shift (the dreaded purple-fade). Doesn't cover scratches you put in the film or rolling the windows down before cure. Warranty transfers if you sell the car.
