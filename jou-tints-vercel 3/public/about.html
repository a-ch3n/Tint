TWENTY YEARS ON GLASS.

Our story

A bay built around the install.

Jou Tints started in 2006 in a single-bay garage off Central Ave. The pitch was simple: stop treating window tint like a side-hustle. Build a clean room. Buy a real plotter. Hire installers who only do glass.

Two decades later, the model holds. Our Minneapolis facility is climate-controlled to keep particulates off the film during cure. Every panel is computer-cut from a database we've been building since 2009. And our techs aren't part-timers — they're full-time tint specialists with an average of nine years on the gun.

We don't wrap, detail, or paint. We tint. That's the entire shop.

How we work

Four rules we don't break.

01

One trade.

We tint. That's it. No paint correction upsells, no detail packages bolted onto your invoice. Specialists beat generalists on glass every time.

02

Computer-cut, never razor-cut.

Every pattern comes off a plotter. No blades touch your factory seals or window gaskets. Period.

03

Clean room, every install.

The bay is filtered, heated, and humidity-controlled year-round. Dust under tint is a contamination failure, not an inevitability.

04

Honest spec sheet.

We tell you what's legal in Minnesota, what your insurance will and won't object to, and what'll get you pulled over. Then we tint.
